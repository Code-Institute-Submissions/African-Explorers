Who are we?

Set in the stunning Okovango Delta in one of the last remaining wild areas on the planet, African Explorers can provide a truly unique safari adventure completely tailored to you needs. Although we can never promise sightings of wild animals we have created a winning formula using a combination of local trackers and scientific researchers as our guides. This has provided us with expert knowledge of the wildlife of the local area due to our guides knowning the lives of the animals intimately. This means we are able to offer unrivalled opportunities to see the unquie fauna & flora of Southern Africa.

Although we pride ourselves on the big 5 of Lion, Buffalo, Elephant, Leopard, and Rhino we also understand that African has so much else to offer and each person has different needs. Birds, mammals, reptiles, whatever your passion we can help.


Some of the other animals the delta is home to include: Crocodile, Hippo, huge numbers of antelope species, specialised wetland birds, snakes, cheetah, wild dogs, Hyena and much much more.


How it works?

We understand that a safari holiday can be a once in a lifetime opportunity which is why we have a team of dedicated specialists. You contact us by telling what you would like to do/see on your trip, length of stay, budget, number of people and any other important information. We then tailor a bespoke holiday for you, matching you with the most relevant guides, accommodation and locations within the delta.
